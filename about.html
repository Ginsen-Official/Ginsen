About Ginsen

Ginsen is a collection of privacy-first browser tools designed for documents, reading, and productivity.


Our goal is simple: Provide useful tools that work directly inside your browser without requiring server-side file processing.


Whether you are merging PDFs, splitting PDFs, or using ReadFlow Reader, your data remains on your device whenever possible.
